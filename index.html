How the SDI works

SDI represents the expected medical severity of a confirmed bite from the identified taxon. The lookup applies the closest supported score in the order species → genus → family → general baseline, and always labels fallbacks.

Read the methodology · View evidence coverage · Run diagnostics

SDI does not predict whether a spider will bite and is not medical advice. Never handle a spider to improve a submission.
